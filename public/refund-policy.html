Refund Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

Our Refund Philosophy

At Ekka Barbershop, we strive to provide excellent service to all our customers. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

Important Notice: Due to the nature of our grooming services, we generally do not offer refunds for completed services. However, we are committed to resolving any issues and ensuring your satisfaction.

Cancellation & Refund Terms

Notice Period Refund/Cancellation Policy
More than 24 hours Full refund or credit for future services
12-24 hours 50% refund or credit
2-12 hours 25% refund or credit
Less than 2 hours No refund - converted to credit for future use
No-show No refund - future booking may require deposit

Service Quality Issues

If you're not satisfied with our service:

  • Complaints must be raised immediately after service completion
  • We will assess the issue and may offer complimentary touch-ups or discounts
  • Serious issues may result in full refunds at management's discretion
  • Photos may be requested to document the concern

Special Circumstances

  • Medical emergencies: Full refund or credit, medical documentation may be required
  • Service unavailability: Full refund if we cancel due to unforeseen circumstances
  • Weather/traffic delays: Considered on case-by-case basis
  • Product allergies: Full refund if not disclosed at booking time

Payment Methods & Refunds

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Cash payments: Refunded in cash or credit
  • Card payments: Refunded to original card
  • Credits expire 6 months from issue date

Loyalty Program Credits

  • Refunded amounts may be converted to loyalty points
  • Points are added at 1 point per SAR spent
  • Points never expire while account is active
